Pre-hospital care and ambulance automation are an unexplored sector in the health care industry.

India witness’s approximately 400 road accident related dearth's every day. Sadly, 54.1% of people killed in road accidents are in the 15-34 years age group.

The Government of India, Government of Rajasthan and the World Bank have signed a US$250 million loan agreement for the Rajasthan State Highways Development Program II Project to build the state's capacity to better manage its highways and improve traffic flows to ensure road safety and to reduce the causalities caused by RTA’s.

. MedYoda with its team of Doctors and Transport Automation experts are closely working with the Tamil Nadu police and IIT Madras. They have designed an ambulance automation project to support IRAD. The proposal is currently under review with IIT Madras.

The project aims in bridging the hospitals, police & Fire to work on a common platform. MedYoda is also working on an AI software that can help us predict the future occurrence of accidents with precise date, time and location.

MedYoda believes this prediction will revolutionize the fields of trauma care and redefine pre-hospital care.

There are countless reasons why MedYoda Chennai is an ideal destination for medical tourism. Unique to the medical tourism sector, MedYoda provides end to end care for its health travelers.

MedYoda has a council of doctors on board who directly communicate with the patient and their Doctors even before they depart from their home country.
